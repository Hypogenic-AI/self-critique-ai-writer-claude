# Downloaded Papers

## Core: Self-Critique for Writing

1. **Writing-Zero: Bridge the Gap Between Non-verifiable Tasks and Verifiable Rewards** (2506.00103)
   - Authors: Jia et al. (Alibaba/Quark LLM), 2025
   - File: `2506.00103_writing_zero.pdf`
   - Why relevant: Most directly relevant — uses pairwise self-critique GenRM + BRPO for writing RL without human feedback

2. **Self-Rewarding Language Models** (2401.10020)
   - Authors: Yuan et al. (Meta/NYU), 2024
   - File: `2401.10020_self_rewarding_language_models.pdf`
   - Why relevant: Foundational self-reward loop via LLM-as-Judge + DPO; writing shows strongest gains

3. **Collective Critics for Creative Story Generation** (2410.02428)
   - Authors: Bae & Kim (UNIST), EMNLP 2024
   - File: `2410.02428_collective_critics_story_gen.pdf`
   - Why relevant: Multi-agent critique framework for story creativity; key counterpoint showing single self-critique may be insufficient

4. **Self-Refine: Iterative Refinement with Self-Feedback** (2303.17651)
   - Authors: Madaan et al. (CMU/Allen AI), 2023
   - File: `2303.17651_self_refine.pdf`
   - Why relevant: Foundational inference-time self-critique loop; ~20% improvement across tasks

5. **PREFINE: Personalized Story Generation via Simulated User Critics** (2510.21721)
   - Authors: 2025
   - File: `2510.21721_prefine_personalized_story.pdf`

6. **EvolvR: Self-Evolving Pairwise Reasoning for Story Evaluation** (2508.06046)
   - Authors: 2025
   - File: `2508.06046_evolvr_story_evaluation.pdf`
   - Why relevant: Self-evolved story evaluator used as reward model for generation improvement
